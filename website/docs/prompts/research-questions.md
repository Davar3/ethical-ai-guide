## Example Output

```
Main Research Question:
RQ1: To what extent does social media marketing influence 
     consumer purchase intentions in the Kurdistan Region?

Sub-Questions:
RQ1a: What is the relationship between social media engagement 
      and brand awareness?
RQ1b: How does perceived authenticity of social media content 
      affect purchase intention?

Hypotheses:
H1: Social media engagement has a significant positive effect 
    on brand awareness.
    H0: Social media engagement has no significant effect on 
        brand awareness.

Variables:
- Independent Variable (IV): Social media marketing activities
- Dependent Variable (DV): Consumer purchase intention
- Mediator: Brand awareness
- Moderator: Age group
```
